Toy ox-drawn cart
क्रीडनक रथः (krīḍanaka rathaḥ)

A toy ox-drawn cart from the Indus Valley Civilisation (c. 2600 BCE) shows that people used bullock carts for transport and trade.

It also suggests that children had toys similar to real-life objects, reflecting daily life in their society.
